The Story

Full of Heart,
Humor & Hope

Meet the Women of the Grieving Widows' Auxiliary who refuse to let grief have the last word. Bound by friendship, faith, and a whole lot of laughter, these widows navigate second chances, new beginnings, and the beautiful messiness of life — together.

Written with warmth and wit by Mildred Dumás, The Grieving Widows' Auxiliary is a celebration of resilience, community, and the joy that blooms in unexpected places. Perfect for every season of life.
